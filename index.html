FAQ

Everything you need to know about the $5 Shield.

Is my contract data safe?

Yes. We use PIPEDA-compliant redaction to strip personal identifiers before any analysis occurs. Your data is encrypted in transit and never stored permanently on our servers.

Is this legal advice?

No. ContractShield provides an AI-powered compliance audit and strategic memo. It is a powerful information tool, not a substitute for a licensed Canadian lawyer. For complex or high-value agreements, always consult a lawyer.

Why is it only $5?

We've automated the associate-level manual review process. By focusing on high-speed statutory checks and AI-driven analysis, we pass the efficiency savings directly to you. The $5 price IS the disruption.

What types of contracts do you support?

ContractShield works with freelance agreements, employment contracts, vendor agreements, NDAs, service agreements, construction contracts, and most standard Canadian business contracts. Upload PDF or Word files.

How long does the analysis take?

Most contracts are fully analysed in under 60 seconds. Complex multi-party agreements may take up to 2 minutes. You receive your full audit package the moment analysis completes.

What format will I receive my results in?

You receive two deliverables: a strategic negotiation memo (PDF) explaining each risk in plain English, and a redlined Word document (.docx) with tracked changes showing exactly which clauses to push back on.

Which provinces does it cover?

ContractShield is calibrated for Canadian federal law and Ontario provincial law, including PIPEDA, the Criminal Code, Employment Standards Act, and Construction Act. Coverage for additional provinces is in development.

Can law firms use this for client work?

Yes. Many small law firms use ContractShield to automate routine clause checks, reducing associate time on standard reviews. Contact us at support@contractshield.ca to discuss firm-level usage and white-labelling options.
